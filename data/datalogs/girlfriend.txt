As the only daughter of the famous Dearests, Girlfriend grew up surrounded by music, fame, and high expectations. Though she often comes off as a bit airheaded, she’s incredibly sharp when it comes to music, always helping Boyfriend refine his sound and polish his songs. Despite her habit of teasing and playfully bullying him (sometimes at the worst moments), her love for him is undeniable. She loves watching Boyfriend perform. And when things go south. She quite rarely steps in. Often letting things play out, sometimes to the detriment of others.
