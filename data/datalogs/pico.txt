Once part of a small-time gang with Nene and Darnell, Pico turned his back on his old friends after learning about the X0 serum, tempted by the promise of power and fame. Street-smart and dangerously skilled. Pico stays paranoid of the police and keeps his distance from authority. Obsessed with guns and quick to fight when cornered, he’s otherwise calm around those he trusts. After using small doses of the X0 serum for an edge, Pico became a top scout and hunter for the X0 clan. He’s crossed paths with Boyfriend a few times in Neo City’s club scene.
