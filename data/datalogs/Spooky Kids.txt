Famous since before they could tie their shoes, Skid and Pump skyrocketed to the top of the charts thanks to their freakishly good voices. By 14 and 15 they're spoiled, smug, and impossible to deal with. Skid's the loud one—reckless, and allergic to authority. He lives for the spotlight and melts down the moment someone else tries to steal it. Pump's quieter, but no less selfish, usually backing Skid up with a sarcastic correction. They're inseparable, and somehow still unsigned. No one knows where their parents are or how they stay out of jail. Honestly? No one really wants to find out.
