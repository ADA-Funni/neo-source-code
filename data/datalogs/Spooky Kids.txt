Best friends since birth, Skid and Pump rose to fame as child prodigies in the music scene. Their loud and energetic performances made them stars before they were even fifteen. but all that early fame went straight to their heads. Now bratty, overconfident, and completely self-centered. Skid is the loud, cocky one who refuses to follow any rules, while Pump is quieter and more grounded, usually backing him up. Despite their success, they’ve stayed independent, avoiding record lables Nobody really knows where their parents are. but somehow, these two have managed to stay out of trouble.. for the most part.
