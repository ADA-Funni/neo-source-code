Raised on the road in his mother’s RV, Boyfriend grew up surrounded by music, lights, and the thrill of live shows. Inspired by the gigs his mom took him to, he found his passion in singing and quickly stood out for his unique, almost unnatural voice. As they traveled the country, his growing talent earned him small spots on stage and a rising fanbase. Eventually, they settled in Neo City, where Boyfriend chased his dream through street performances and late-night club gigs. It was there he met Girlfriend, his biggest supporter, duet partner, and love of his life.
