A demonic rockstar and powerful music mogul, Daddy Dearest dominates Neo City's music scene. Once a chart-topping performer, he now runs the industry from behind the scenes, signing artists, hosting rap battles, and producing hit videos. his influence only grew stronger with time. Alongside his equally demonic wife, he bends every rule to his advantage, confident that no one can touch them. Though age has dulled his voice, his pride refuses to admit it, clinging to the spotlight that made him a legend. He also owns a chain of bars where rising stars fight for their chance to shine, on his terms.
