Once a man with dreams of becoming the greatest voice the world had ever heard, he vanished from the public eye at the height of his fame. No one knows what happened to him. Rumors speak of a secret obsession—whispers of experiments, of something he found deep in the underbelly of Neo City. He became something else. Something... Inhuman. Sightings of a twisted figure with a warped grin and a hunger. His name unknown. But what's left of the man inside is anyone's guess. He's looking for someone. Hunting. To fix a mistake. Others say it's something worse. No one dares to ask him.
